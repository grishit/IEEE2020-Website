Research Areas

Working on Non- Isolated Topologies for photovoltaic systems. Photo-voltaic is a renewable energy source which is abundant in India, main problem associated with its utilization of PV panel is the maximum power evacuation from it. Thus, PE interface becomes a requisite for controlling the power evacuation from PV panel. Non- Isolated converters provide a better solution for low power application like rooftop solution.
